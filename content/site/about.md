I've spent over 25 years in data architecture, long enough to know which trends are worth pursuing and which are noise. I'm a pragmatic generalist who looks at the whole picture: the technology, the business case, and the human side of change that most engagements ignore.

I founded **Insidin** because organisations that are stuck deserve frank, experience-backed advice, not another framework presentation.

I created the **DG2DMM framework** because existing maturity models measure compliance, not outcomes. DG2DMM ties data governance to measurable value and drives it through the platform itself.
