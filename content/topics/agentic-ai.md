Agentic AI represents a step change from prompt-response interactions to autonomous, goal-directed systems that can plan, execute, and adapt. The practical implications for enterprise architecture, governance, and risk management are significant, and largely underexplored.

This topic covers the practical reality of agentic AI: what works today, what the real risks are (not the speculative ones), and how organisations should think about integrating autonomous AI capabilities into their data and technology landscape.
