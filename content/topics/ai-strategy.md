Most AI strategy fails not because it's absent, but because it expires before it can be executed. The landscape moves too fast for static roadmaps. Organisations need living strategy mechanisms that adapt continuously, grounded in honest risk/value analysis rather than vendor promises or hype cycles.

This topic collects thinking on AI strategy, readiness assessment, and the critical evaluation of what AI investments are actually worth pursuing, drawing on applied experience across enterprise and startup contexts.
