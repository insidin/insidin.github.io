Most organisations approach data governance as a compliance exercise: policies, stewardship committees, and cataloguing efforts that look impressive on paper but change nothing about how data is actually used.

DG2DMM was built to break that pattern. It ties every governance activity to a measurable business outcome, prioritises investments based on where maturity actually needs to grow, and drives governance through the data platform itself rather than relying on manual processes that decay over time.

This topic collects the thinking, frameworks, and practical tools behind outcome-driven data governance.
